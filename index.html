Background and Context

Objectives

Research Question: Do water inputs influence zooplankton composition at various locations in Lake Matoaka?

Null Hypothesis: The zooplankton composition is similar among our five sample sites.

Alternative Hypothesis: The zooplankton composition is different among our five sample sites.

Method

  • Canoed out to each location
  • At each site, threw out plankton net 3 times per deposit for 1-2 deposits (until the bottle was at least 2/3 full)
  • Preserved samples with ethanol and stored in fridge
  • Photographed plankton with phone cameras through microscopes (lense magnifications?) for each new organism observed - We looked at 6 slides per sample site (3 slides per person)
  • Compared images to a variety of online sources to identify plankton
